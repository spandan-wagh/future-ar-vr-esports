# Future AR/VR Technology

## Overview

This repository documents my independent research into the future of augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), artificial intelligence (AI), haptics, and their applications in competitive gaming.

The goal is to understand current technologies, identify limitations, and explore ideas that could shape the next generation of immersive experiences.

## Research Areas

- Augmented Reality (AR)
- Virtual Reality (VR)
- Artificial Intelligence
- Haptics
- Human-Computer Interaction
- Competitive Gaming
- Esports
